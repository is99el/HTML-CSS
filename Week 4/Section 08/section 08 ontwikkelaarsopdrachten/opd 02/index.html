Container Properities

//Creates a grid display:grid || inline-grid;
//Creates a grid columns grid-templete-columns: length || percentage || fr || line-name
//Creates a grid rows grid-templete-rows: length || percentage || fr || line-name
//Creates a grid with specified areas grid-templete-ares: grid-arena-name|| none ||
//Creates rows, colums and areas in a single declaration grid-template: none(initial values) || grid-template-rows/grid-template-columns
//Creates spaces between grid-items on the row axis column-gap (grid-column-gap) : length
//Creates spaces between grid-items on the column axis gap (grid-gap) : grid-row-gap grid-column-gap
//Align grid items along the inline/ row axis justify-items: start || end || center || stretsch
//Align grid items along the block/ column axis align-items: start || end || center || stretsch
//Align grid items along the inline and block axis (ahorthand) place-items: align-items / justify-items
//Align the whole grid (container) along the inline / row axis justify-content: start || end || center || stretsch || space-around || space-between || space-evenly
//Align the whole grid (container) along the block / row axis justify-content: start || end || center || stretsch || space-around || space-between || space-evenly
//Align the whole grid (container) along the block / block axis align-content: start || end || center || stretsch || space-around || space-between || space-evenly
//Align the whole grid (container) along the inline and block axis (ahorthand) place-content: align-content/ justify-content
//Specifies the size of any auto-genereated grid row grid-auto-rows: size(lenght) || percentage || fr
//Specifies the size of any auto-genereated grid column grid-auto-rows: size(lenght) || percentage || fr
//Sets the algorithm that places the grid-items grid-auto-flow: row || column || dense
//Shorthand for almost every property grid: grid-template-row grid-template-columns grid-template-areas grid-auto-flow grid-auto-rows grid-auto-columns
